All about the vibe

  • 1 king bed or 2 twin beds
  • 1 shower & bathtub
  • 2 adults
  • 52 m2 / 560 sq.ft.
  • Mountain view

The 52 sqm Mountain View Suites, housed on the upper level of the main hotel building, are perfect mountain hideaways offering supreme comfort and spectacular views. Designed to inspire relaxation, the suites combine contemporary décor with local accents, and modern amenities including a freestanding bathtub, separate rain shower, Wi-Fi and flatscreen TV. Take in the full majesty of the surroundings from a private balcony offering the most dramatic views over the pool and down into the gorge.
